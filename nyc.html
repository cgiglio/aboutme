Growing up in New York

Needless to say, I didn't have a backyard

Born in the Weill Cornell Medical Center in Manhattan on May 24th, 1995, I was a New Yorker from Day 1. I've lived all my life in the same apartment building; we moved from the 15th to the 39th floor when I was 8 a few short years after the birth of Matthew. My parents enrolled me in the Garden House School, a Pre-K just 3 blocks from my house.

When it came time for kindergarten, my parents interviewed me at over 8 schools. I eventually chose Collegiate, a K-12 private school on the Upper West Side.
