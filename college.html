College Life For Chris

Baseballing, COS'ing, Praying

As you've probably figured out by now, I'm a Computer Science Major (affectionately called COS here at Princeton) and a member of the Varsity Baseball team. What you probably haven't learned yet is that I'm also an active member of Princeton Faith & Action, the largest Christian group on campus. I currently lead their Communications/Creativity team and help do outreach via email and social media.

I've had the opportunity to take classes from all different disciplines: economics, statisitcs, information security, computer graphics, Ancient Rome, comic books, and sustainable design. For my first two years, I was placed into Forbes College, a residental collge assigned to roughly one in six underclassmen. Unbelievibly, it's actually the only University housing that's actually off campus!
