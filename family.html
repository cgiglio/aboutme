Dad

My Dad was born in Calabria, Italy, and immigrated to the US when he was 4. He moved to Mount Vernon, New York, where he was an all-state soccer player. He went on to major in Electrical and Biomedical Engineering at Duke University and went on to Dental School. He is now a Prosthodontist in New York.

Mom

My Mom was born in Havana, Cuba and successfully immigrated to the US when she was 4 (just like my Dad). She also moved to New York State and settled into Hollis Hills, Queens. She eventually went to New York University and graduated as a Chemistry major, going on to Dental School. She is now a Periodontist in New York in the same practice as my father

Alex

Alex, the middle brother of my family, is a senior in High School at the same school I attended, Collegiate. In addition to being the captain of the Varsity Baseball Team, Alex is a member of student government, graphic designer for the yearbook, and self-professed master bread-baker

Matthew

Matthew, affectionately known as "Mip" by my family, is a freshmen at Collegiate as well. Carrying on what seems to be the family tradition, Matthew also plays baseball and is an outfielder on the Varsity Baseball Team. When he's not working hard at school and sports, he manages to make an extremely large mess at home.
